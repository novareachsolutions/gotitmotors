FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What makes Got It Motors the best used car dealership?

Got It Motors is renowned for its extensive inventory of quality used cars, transparent pricing, and exceptional customer service. We go the extra mile to ensure every vehicle meets the highest standards, providing peace of mind to our customers.

2. Why should I choose a used car dealership over a private seller?

Buying from a dealership offers numerous benefits including warranty protection, thorough vehicle inspections, proper documentation, and consumer protection laws that don't apply to private sales.

3. What types of vehicles are available at your second-hand car dealership?

We offer a wide selection of vehicles including sedans, SUVs, hatchbacks, utes, and more. Our inventory is constantly updated with quality pre-owned vehicles to suit all budgets and preferences.

4. Is it safe to buy a used car from a dealership?

Absolutely! At Got It Motors, every vehicle undergoes a comprehensive 150-point inspection. We provide complete vehicle history reports and offer warranty coverage for your peace of mind.

5. How does Got It Motors ensure the quality of its used cars for sale?

Every vehicle in our inventory goes through a rigorous 150-point inspection process covering mechanical, electrical, and cosmetic aspects. Only vehicles that meet our high standards make it to our showroom floor.

6. Can I test drive a vehicle before purchasing it at your car dealership?

Yes, absolutely! We encourage all our customers to test drive any vehicle they're interested in. Simply contact us to book a test drive at your convenience.
