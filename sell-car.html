Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about selling your car with Got It Motors.

1. How can I sell my car through Got It Motors?

Selling your car with Got It Motors is simple. Just provide your car details using our online form or visit our location for an inspection. Our team will assess your vehicle and provide a competitive offer on the spot.

2. What types of cars do you buy?

We buy all types of cars including sedans, SUVs, hatchbacks, wagons, utes, and more. Whether your car is in excellent condition or needs some work, we're interested in making you an offer.

3. Do I need to prepare my car before selling it?

While not required, giving your car a basic clean can help make a good impression. You don't need a roadworthy certificate - we handle all the inspection requirements.

4. How is my car's value determined?

We consider multiple factors including make, model, year, mileage, condition, service history, and current market demand. Our network of 1000+ licensed buyers ensures you get competitive offers.

5. Can I sell my car if it still has finance on it?

Yes, you can sell a car with existing finance. We'll work with your finance company to settle the outstanding amount. Any remaining balance after settlement will be paid to you.

6. How long does the selling process take?

The process is quick! You can receive genuine offers within hours of listing. Once you accept an offer, payment and pickup can often be arranged within days.

7. Why should I sell my car to Got It Motors instead of privately?

Selling privately involves dealing with strangers, negotiating, handling paperwork, and security risks. With Got It Motors, you get access to 1000+ licensed buyers, we handle negotiations, and you avoid scammers. It's faster, safer, and often results in better prices.
